Twa Andras

Or "2 Andrews". Or "Andy & Drew". Or even "fanny-baws an' heid-tha-baw". Just like our custom whisky bottles, you can call us whatever you want.

How but?

We want people to enjoy whisky without fear. To explore Scotch and to have fun doing so. Drink whatever you like and however you like it. But drink responsibly, and with good company.

Head to the blending lab and fill your bottle with the whiskies that sound good to you. Once your bottle is full you can name it and declare yourself as the blender. Just have fun and trust your instincts!

Oor story

The idea came one day after we'd enjoyed a few drams at a whisky festival in Ayr (and perhaps enjoyed it slightly more than we should have). After some food and a snooze on the train back to Glasgow we went for a few pints—and that’s when the idea came to us! This concept of allowing people to have their very own bottle of Scotch... through a website!

We were so excited! We discussed it more, drew up sketches, concept mocks, the works. Slowly but surely life got in the way and this idea was put back in the whisky cabinet until such a time arose that we said to each other "let’s do it". And we did. Whisky Blender was officially established in 2011... and we've been having a dram guid time ever since!
